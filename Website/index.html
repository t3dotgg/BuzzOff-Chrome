What is BuzzOff?

BuzzOff is the best quick answer to BuzzFeed, Upworthy, FaithIt, and all that other clickbait that clogs up your newsfeed. Created as an open source HackRPI Fall 2014 project, BuzzOff was created in 24 hours as a Chrome extension.
